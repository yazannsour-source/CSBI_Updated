Welcome to CSBI

A Platform Dedicated to Sustainable Transformation

The Centre for Sustainable Business and Innovation (CSBI) is a forward-looking platform dedicated to advancing sustainable economic transformation through innovation, research, and collaboration.

Positioned at the intersection of business, technology, and sustainability, CSBI catalyzes building resilient, inclusive, and environmentally responsible economies aligned with Saudi Vision 2030.

As Saudi Arabia accelerates its transformation, CSBI responds to the need for institutions that bridge knowledge, innovation, and implementation.

Why CSBI Matters

Responding to Saudi Arabia's Historic Transformation

Saudi Arabia is undergoing a historic shift toward diversification, innovation, and sustainability. Mega-projects like NEOM and national initiatives such as the Saudi Green Initiative highlight the Kingdom's commitment to environmental leadership and economic transformation.

  • ✓ Supporting businesses in adopting sustainable practices
  • ✓ Enabling innovation ecosystems that drive economic growth
  • ✓ Developing talent equipped to lead sustainability transitions
  • ✓ Advancing research that informs policy and industry
