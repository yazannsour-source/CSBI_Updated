Strategic Goals

Driving Measurable Impact

Tangible outcomes that support economic transformation and sustainability — aligned with Saudi Vision 2030 and global sustainability frameworks.

Our Six Goals

Strategic Goals 2025–2030

These goals contribute directly to economic diversification, sustainability leadership, and innovation-driven growth — aligned with both national and global priorities.

01

Advance Sustainable Business Research

Develop cutting-edge research that addresses key challenges such as climate change, resource efficiency, and digital transformation.

02

Enable Innovation Ecosystems

Support the development of innovation ecosystems that foster entrepreneurship, technology development, and commercialization.

03

Strengthen Industry Collaboration

Build strong partnerships with businesses to co-create solutions and drive real-world implementation at scale.

04

Influence Policy & Governance

Provide evidence-based insights to support policymaking and regulatory development at national and regional levels.

05

Build Human Capital

Develop skills and capabilities through education and training programs for professionals across sectors.

06

Foster Global Partnerships

Collaborate with leading international institutions to exchange knowledge, best practices, and co-develop solutions.

In Depth

Goals in Detail

01

Advance Sustainable Business Research

We invest in frontier research that generates insights applicable across business, policy, and society. By addressing pressing challenges — from climate risk and resource scarcity to digital disruption — our research outputs directly support national and global sustainability agendas.

02

Enable Innovation Ecosystems

We cultivate environments where entrepreneurs, researchers, and industry leaders collaborate to develop and commercialize sustainable innovations. Through innovation labs and startup incubation, we accelerate the journey from concept to market-ready solution.

03

Strengthen Industry Collaboration

We build structured, long-term partnerships with corporations, SMEs, and public institutions to co-develop tools, strategies, and innovations that respond to real market and societal needs — ensuring our work achieves tangible, lasting impact.

Key Performance Indicators

CSBI measures its impact through a set of clear, quantifiable KPIs that reflect progress toward each strategic goal and our broader mission.

  • Number of research publications
  • Industry partnerships established
  • Innovation projects launched
  • Training programs delivered
  • Policy contributions made
