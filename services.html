Our Services

Expert Solutions for Sustainable Transformation

From advisory consulting and executive training to innovation labs and policy engagement — CSBI delivers practical, high-impact services to organizations navigating the sustainability transition.

What We Offer

Six Core Service Areas

CSBI's services are designed to bridge the gap between knowledge and implementation — translating research, expertise, and innovation into tangible outcomes for your organization.

Sustainability Consulting & Advisory

Strategic guidance for organizations embedding sustainability into core operations, governance, and long-term planning.

Executive Training & Capacity Building

Bespoke programs equipping leaders and teams with the skills to drive sustainability and innovation initiatives.

Applied Research & Insights

Commissioned, industry-focused research delivering actionable intelligence on sustainability, ESG, and digital transformation.

Innovation Lab & Startup Support

Structured incubation, mentorship, and prototyping support for startups and teams developing sustainability-driven solutions.

Policy Engagement & Advocacy

Evidence-based policy analysis and advisory services helping government bodies design effective sustainability frameworks.

Global Partnerships & Benchmarking

International collaboration, comparative analysis, and benchmarking against global sustainability standards and best practices.

Service 01
Consulting & Advisory

Sustainability Strategy & ESG Advisory

CSBI's consulting team works directly with organizations — from multinational corporations to Saudi SMEs — to develop comprehensive sustainability strategies, ESG roadmaps, and implementation plans aligned with global frameworks and Vision 2030.

Our advisors bring deep expertise across environmental management, corporate governance, stakeholder engagement, and regulatory compliance.

  • Sustainability strategy development and gap analysis
  • ESG materiality assessment and reporting (GRI, SASB, TCFD)
  • Carbon footprint measurement and net-zero roadmaps
  • Sustainable supply chain assessment and optimization
  • Stakeholder engagement and communication strategies
Request a Consultation
Service 02
Training & Education

Executive Training & Capacity Building Programs

Developed for senior executives, mid-level managers, and emerging leaders, CSBI's training programs are designed around real-world challenges and delivered by expert faculty with extensive industry and research backgrounds.

Programs range from intensive workshops to multi-module leadership academies, available in-person, online, or hybrid format.

  • Sustainable Leadership Academy (6-module executive program)
  • ESG Fundamentals for Board Directors and Senior Executives
  • Digital Sustainability and AI for Business Leaders
  • Green Finance and Impact Investment Certification
  • Tailored in-house training programs for corporate teams
View All Programs
Service 03
Applied Research

Commissioned Research & Market Insights

Organizations partner with CSBI to commission rigorous, applied research that delivers actionable intelligence tailored to their strategic needs. Our multidisciplinary research teams bring together expertise across sustainability, finance, digital transformation, and policy.

Research outputs range from market landscape reports and benchmarking studies to deep-dive white papers and policy briefs.

  • Industry-specific sustainability landscape and maturity reports
  • ESG benchmarking and peer comparison studies
  • Consumer and stakeholder perception surveys
  • Regulatory impact assessments and policy briefs
  • ESBR journal collaboration and co-authorship
Explore Our Research
Service 04
Innovation & Startups

Innovation Lab & Startup Incubation

CSBI's Innovation Lab is a collaborative space where startups, corporate innovators, and researchers come together to experiment, prototype, and scale sustainability-driven solutions. We provide structured support throughout the innovation lifecycle.

Startups receive access to mentorship from CSBI faculty, PMU's research infrastructure, and connections to a regional investor and industry network.

  • Structured 12-week incubation program with mentorship
  • Access to PMU facilities, labs, and digital infrastructure
  • Pitch coaching and investor introductions
  • Co-development partnerships with industry players
  • Proof-of-concept funding support and pilot testing
Apply to the Lab
