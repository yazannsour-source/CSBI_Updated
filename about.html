About CSBI

Who We Are

A multidisciplinary hub committed to advancing sustainability through innovation-led business transformation — integrating academic rigor with real-world application.

Our Purpose

Ideas, Expertise & Innovation Converge

The global economy is undergoing a profound transformation driven by climate challenges, technological disruption, and evolving societal expectations. In Saudi Arabia, this transformation is amplified by the ambitions of Saudi Vision 2030, which calls for:

  • → Economic diversification
  • → Environmental sustainability
  • → Private sector growth
  • → Knowledge-based development

CSBI was established to contribute directly to these objectives — adopting a systems-thinking approach that recognizes sustainability challenges are interconnected and require integrated solutions across energy transition, sustainable urban development, digital transformation, and inclusive economic growth.

Core Activities

How We Create Impact

1

Research

We conduct interdisciplinary research that draws on multiple fields — science, economics, and social studies — to generate well-rounded insights. Our findings inform public policy, shape business strategy, and translate research outcomes into practical applications and new technologies.

2

Innovation

We develop and pilot scalable solutions designed to tackle pressing real-world challenges across various sectors. By piloting these solutions, we gather valuable data to refine their effectiveness. Once validated, they are designed to scale efficiently — ensuring innovative ideas transition into sustainable, real-world applications.

3

Engagement

We collaborate closely with partners across industry, government, and civil society to create meaningful and lasting impact. Our partnerships translate ideas into market-ready applications, support effective policy development, and ensure community needs are fully represented in our initiatives.

4

Education

We build capacity by equipping individuals and organizations with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in a rapidly changing environment — through targeted training programs, executive education, and knowledge-sharing platforms across sectors.

5

Publication

The Centre publishes a bi-annual journal — Environment, Society and Business Review (ESBR) — a double-blind, peer-reviewed, open-access multidisciplinary journal committed to disseminating impactful research to a global audience of scholars, practitioners, and policymakers.
