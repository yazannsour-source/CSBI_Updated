Core Values

Guiding Principles of CSBI

The values that define how we operate, collaborate, and create impact — aligned with global best practices and the national priorities of Saudi Vision 2030.

Our Values

Embedded in Everything We Do

Our values are embedded in research methodologies, industry collaborations, educational programs, and organizational culture — guiding every decision we make.

Innovation with Purpose

We believe innovation should address real-world challenges and deliver measurable impact. Our work focuses on creating solutions that are not only creative but also scalable and sustainable.

Collaboration & Partnership

Bringing together diverse stakeholders — industry leaders, researchers, policymakers, and communities — to co-create solutions that are impactful and scalable. Sustainability challenges cannot be solved in isolation.

Integrity & Responsibility

We uphold the highest standards of ethics, transparency, and accountability in all aspects of our work — creating long-term societal value by aligning our efforts with the broader needs of communities and future generations.

Inclusivity & Diversity

We embrace diverse perspectives and experiences, understanding that a wide range of viewpoints leads to stronger and more creative outcomes — making solutions more effective, equitable, and relevant to diverse communities.

Systems Thinking

We approach challenges holistically, recognizing that economic, environmental, and social systems are deeply interconnected — developing balanced, sustainable, and resilient solutions that account for ripple effects across all dimensions.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in research, innovation, and engagement — maintaining high standards in both quality and relevance, positioning CSBI as a leading centre of expertise recognized for its contributions and influence.

Collaboration

Partnership as a Core Value

Collaboration and partnership are at the heart of driving sustainable business transformation and meaningful innovation. Our centre fosters an ecosystem where shared knowledge and collective action accelerate progress toward sustainability goals. We foster partnerships across:

  • Academia — for rigorous research and knowledge creation
  • Industry — for translating ideas into market-ready applications
  • Government — for effective policy development and implementation
  • Civil society — for ensuring community needs are represented
Values in Action

How Values Shape Our Work

Research Methodologies

Our values guide how we design studies, select methodologies, and report findings with rigor and transparency.

Industry Collaborations

Every partnership is built on shared commitment to integrity, mutual benefit, and long-term impact.

Educational Programs

Programs are designed to foster inclusive, excellence-driven learning that prepares leaders for systemic change.

Organizational Culture

Internally, our values shape how we work together — with respect, openness, and collective purpose.

Global Sustainability Goals

All activities are oriented toward contributing to the UN SDGs and Saudi Vision 2030 national priorities.

Decision-Making

Every strategic decision is evaluated against our values — ensuring alignment between ambition and responsibility.
